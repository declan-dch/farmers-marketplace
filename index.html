Welcome to Local Farm Trading Post
We want to provide a unique experience for Homesteaders and local farms. With our website, you can find local farmers in the Fulton-Montgomery area to trade with. Trade eggs for bread, wheat for corn, pork for steak. Whatever you want, and others offer.
